Our Modules

Route Optimization
Planrota's Route Optimization Module is an advanced algorithm that analyzes various parameters, such as distance, staff capacities and vehicle capacities, to determine the most efficient and cost-effective routes for businesses. By optimizing the routing process, our module helps companies reduce transportation costs, minimize delivery times, and maximize sales opportunities. Our platform ensures that field personnel are deployed in the most efficient manner. By leveraging the power of our Route Optimization Module, businesses can enhance their operational efficiency, improve customer satisfaction, and increase profitability. Our module is designed to be flexible and scalable, enabling businesses of all sizes to benefit from optimized routing and reduced costs
Account Management
Planrota's comprehensive platform offers web and mobile applications that provide seamless integration of warehouse and inventory management. With a focus on streamlining operations, our platform optimizes order and collection management, allowing for maximum productivity and efficiency for field personnel. Our integrated system ensures that inventory levels are accurately tracked and orders are fulfilled quickly and accurately. By utilizing Planrota's platform, businesses can reduce errors, minimize delays, and ultimately enhance customer satisfaction. With real-time access to inventory levels and order statuses, businesses can make informed decisions and respond quickly to changing demands.Planrota's platform is designed to meet the unique needs of businesses of all sizes, making it a reliable solution for effective inventory and order management.
Reports
Planrota offers a comprehensive suite of reporting tools that draw on our extensive field work experience to deliver real-time insights into critical business operations. Our platform features pre-built reports that provide businesses with detailed information on key performance indicators related to inventory, sales, collection, and route optimization. In addition, our platform allows businesses to design dynamic forms and customized reports that are tailored to their unique needs and requirements. By leveraging the power of our reporting tools, businesses can gain deeper insights into their operations, identify areas for improvement, and make data-driven decisions that drive growth and profitability. Our platform is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, making it easy for businesses to access the information they need to succeed. With Planrota, businesses can unlock the full potential of their data to optimize their operations and achieve their goals.
